I am Juan Diego Bogotá, a ninth semester student of systems engineering and design at the Universidad de los Andes. I like everything that has to do with video game development, animation, 3d modeling and mobile development plus I'm a fan of soccer, movies, video games and technology. I have worked as an instructor at the university and with an electric skateboard company developing all their branding.
